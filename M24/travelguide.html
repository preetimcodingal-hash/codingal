My Travel Gallery

The Eiffel Tower, Paris
The Eiffel Tower is one of the most famous landmarks in the world, located in Paris, France. It was built in 1889 and stands 324 meters tall.
The Taj Mahal, India
The Taj Mahal in Agra, India, is a symbol of love. It was built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal and is made of white marble.
The Great Wall of China, China
The Great Wall of China is one of the longest structures ever built. It stretches over 21,000 km and was constructed to protect China from invasions.
